Events

Events in College Station bring the community and visitors together all year long. From live music and local markets to sporting events, festivals and family-friendly experiences, there is always something happening in the Heart of Aggieland. Whether you are planning a weekend visit or looking to experience the city like a local, College Station’s event calendar offers memorable moments that showcase its energy, traditions and unmistakable sense of place.
Depending on the time of year, take advantage of vibrant happenings in entertainment, sports, food, the arts and more.

Hosting an event in College Station? Whether it is a festival, concert, market or community gathering, we would love to help spread the word. Local businesses and event organizers are invited to submit their event details to be featured on our calendar and shared with visitors and locals alike.
